Game Of Thrones Review and Analysis

Season 1

Review & Analysis

The first season has great start setting up the core for the show, the families and their motives
and provide us with little insight of the world Of The Westroes and its kingdoms. Generating
required curiosity and intrest in the dymanics of the things at stake and most of all ends really
well leaving the audience in shock and eager for the upcoming dramas.

Season 2

Review & Analysis

The season 2 gets to the great start where we were left in the previous one , in this season the
main antagonist is reveled - The night king beyond the wall making us feel things in the westroas
like they dont matter but the politics and the family dramas never lets you down. This season also
establishes the heros of the Show "The Starks"

Season 3

Review & Analysis

The season 3 of the game of thrones has one of the most shocking and saddest endings the
television history makes you doubt the direction of show were it might be going , it almost breaks
your heart after witnessing such brutal and gore scenes . this season has capacity to catch you
off guard

Season 4

Review & Analysis

Season 4 of the game of thrones is the best season of the series according the rates at the IMDb
and that is for a reason the cast does fantastic job , peter dinklage stands above all does the
best work of his life. The story starts shaping and most the plots reaches intersection
. The season reveals of few old and new influential families of the westroes
